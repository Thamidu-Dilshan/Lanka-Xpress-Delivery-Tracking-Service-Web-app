About Us

The company was established in April 2015 as a brainchild of a few professionals with 10 to 30 years of experience in the domestic courier and ICT industry. At inception, Prompt Xpress recruited a team of high-performing personnel to serve all regions of the country. With its strong ICT backing, Prompt Xpress is positioned to be a leading domestic courier provider in a short period.

The company was founded to address a gap in providing efficient, trustworthy, and customer-friendly courier services to public and private sector organizations across the island. Prompt Xpress operates with an island-wide branch network for its exclusive clientele.

We are committed to offering the most customer-focused services with support from innovative ICT applications. We already boast a state-of-the-art tracking system and plan to introduce mobile handheld and tracking applications.
